Q & A Testing

QA & SOFTWARE TESTING SERVICES

Turn to Dimy’s QA Services for a helping hand in your software development journey. We’ll help you strategize and plan for your product’s life cycle, monitor all phases of development and provide you with product quality feedback.

BENEFITS OF LEVERAGING OUR QA SERVICES


Receive helping hands and experience from our team for ensured quality of your software with less workload from your team!


Saves Time & Costs

It can take up to 100 times longer to repair an error on a deployed software compared to repairing it during its development stage. By having thorough QA Processes, bugs can be spotted before the software is ready for deployment.

Data Safety

Without proper quality assurance done with your software, there may be architecture errors that may not be visible to the ordinary eye, but hackers are able to detect the software’s weaknesses. It’s crucial for the software to have a solid foundation.

Protect Your Reputation

It will be your responsibility to deliver a proper reliable software for your clients, however when the results aren’t up to standard (Errors / Data Leaks), your reputation may tank, clients will then lose their trust in you for offering poor standards which may result in sharing their negative experiences with other potential clients.

Ensures Long Term Profits

When costs are minimized, reputation is protected, and software standards are met, the extra funds that aren’t used to fix unwanted errors can be allocated into growing your business’s revenue and profits.

WHAT IS OUR QA SERVICE CYCLE LIKE?

Our team of QA testers will be working closely with you from the early stages of your product development. This ensures that we all will work harmoniously and efficiently towards delivering

Our team will conduct a thorough assessment of your project documentation, management processes and QA related issues faced by your team.
Once we have done the necessary studies on your current project requirements and revealed the need for a QA process redesign, we’ll be providing you with solutions to the existing issues, model a new and more effective QA process and roadmap for optimal results.
After we are all agreeable to the new suggested QA process and roadmap, we’ll be implementing the new processes.
Once the new QA process has been completed, our test engineers will conduct another round of testing to scan through hidden bugs and perform fixes on errors that are discovered.
When the software’s functionality is stable and previous errors has been fixed, the team will perform a final test and issue a test result report once the process is completed.
